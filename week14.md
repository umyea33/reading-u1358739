Mocking something for a test is very similar to the Digital Twin Universe.  They are both taking somthing and turnining it into this fake or "mocked" version of the same thing in order to test something more easily.  Usually mocks are a lot smaller that something like the Digital Twin Universe, but it is still the exact same idea.  The Digital Twin Universe seems like a big artifact of mocked functionality and the typical way mocks are used is for just one tiny slice of what the Digital Twin Universe does.  I think it is very likely that someone would use both.  They might use mocks for simple little things that they need and the Digital Twin Universe for something bigger like google docs.
